Christoffel Symbols: 

\[ 
\Gamma_{a,b}^m 
	= \dfrac{g^{mn}}{2}(\partial_b g_{an}+\partial_a g_{bn}-\partial_n g_{ab})
	= \dfrac{1}{2} \left( 
			\dfrac{\partial g_{na}}{\partial x^b}
		+ \dfrac{\partial g_{nb}}{\partial x^a}
		- \dfrac{\partial g_{ab}}{\partial x^n}
\right)
\]
